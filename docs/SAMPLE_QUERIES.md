# Sample Medical Research Queries

Use these example prompts to test the assistant. Rephrase as needed for your specialty.

## Cardiology
- What is the efficacy of SGLT2 inhibitors in heart failure with preserved ejection fraction?
- Recent RCT evidence on colchicine for secondary prevention after myocardial infarction

## Endocrinology
- Meta-analysis evidence for metformin in gestational diabetes
- GLP-1 receptor agonists and major adverse cardiovascular events in type 2 diabetes

## Hypertension
- First-line antihypertensive therapy in adults under 55 — recent randomized trial evidence
- Renal outcomes with finerenone in chronic kidney disease and type 2 diabetes

## Infectious Disease
- Efficacy of nirmatrelvir-ritonavir in vaccinated high-risk COVID-19 outpatients

## Oncology (research framing)
- Immunotherapy plus chemotherapy vs chemotherapy alone in advanced NSCLC — systematic review findings

## Expected behavior
- Answers cite PubMed-retrieved papers with title, journal, year, and link.
- If abstracts are sparse or off-topic, the system returns:
  **"Current evidence is insufficient to provide a reliable answer."**
- Emergency or personal treatment prompts are blocked by safety guardrails.
